Scope of the Event

This international symposium gathers researchers to discuss state-of-the-art developments in algal and organic waste biorefineries. These sustainable systems aim to develop integrated technologies for valorizing organic waste, including hydrothermal liquefaction (HTL) for fuel and chemical production. The event also covers low-carbon fuels and the growing bioeconomy, supporting climate change mitigation through low carbon technologies.

How to Reach

By Air

The distance from Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport (GAU) to IIT Guwahati is about 22 km. In the Arrival Lounge (inside Airport), there is a pre-paid taxi counter. You can take a prepaid taxi to reach IIT Guwahati campus.

Airport (GAU) ~22 km Prepaid taxi

By Train

Guwahati Railway Station (GHY) to IIT Guwahati is about 18 km. If arriving at GHY, come out through Platform No.1 exit gate (Panbazar/Lakhtokia side). A limited number of IIT buses run from Lakhtokia (near Don Bosco School's Church / Cotton College Boys Hostel) to IIT campus.

Kamakhya Railway Station (KYQ) is about 13 km from IIT Guwahati; for commuting you may need taxi/auto-rickshaw.

GHY ~18 km IIT buses (limited)
